Inani beach

Inani Beach, part of Cox's Bazar Beach, is an 18-kilometre-long sea beach in Ukhia Upazila of Cox's Bazar District, Bangladesh. It has a lot of coral stones, which are very sharp. These coral stones look black and green, and they are found in summer or rainy seasons. Pathorkhani is located in Jaliapalong, Inani Beach.
